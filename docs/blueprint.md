# **App Name**: EBENESAID

## Core Features:

- Personalized Relocation Dashboard: A central, mobile-first hub for students to track their relocation progress, access dynamically updated checklists, and receive tailored guidance based on their nationality, university, and arrival status. This feature leverages a context-aware onboarding tool to ensure relevant information.
- Verified Accommodation Marketplace: Browse and secure verified housing options. The platform uses an AI-powered matching tool to connect students with accommodation based on price, proximity, and preferences, along with landlord verification for trust.
- Document Wallet & Administrative Checklists: A secure digital wallet for storing critical documents and interactive administrative checklists that automatically remind students of upcoming deadlines and required steps for residence permits, SIM cards, transport, and more.
- Cultural Onboarding & Local Guide: Access interactive resources on Latvian culture, social norms, public safety, healthcare access, scam awareness, and basic language phrases. This module provides essential information for cultural integration.
- Curated Job & Internship Board: Discover vetted part-time job and internship opportunities from trusted Latvian and pan-Baltic employers, specifically tailored for international students and integrating employer placement fees for sustainability.
- Community & Buddy Matching: Connect with other international students and peer ambassadors through an AI-powered social matching tool based on shared university, country of origin, and interests to foster community and belonging. Provides access to organized social events.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme that emphasizes professionalism, clarity, and trust. Primary accent color: A deep, reassuring blue (#2270CC). This hue, inspired by concepts of 'structure' and 'help', provides stability. The background is a highly desaturated, cool white (#EBF1F7), ensuring clean aesthetics and optimal readability. A vibrant teal-green accent (#33E0E0) adds a refreshing contrast, highlighting key actions and information.
- Primary font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for all text. Chosen for its exceptional readability, modern and objective aesthetic, it ensures a clean and user-friendly experience across headlines and body content for an international audience.
- Use a set of clear, universally understandable line icons with a minimalist design to guide international users intuitively through complex administrative tasks and service options.
- Employ a mobile-first, grid-based layout with generous white space to present information clearly and prevent overwhelm. Modular components allow for easy scaling and content customization, emphasizing logical flow and ease of navigation.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for feedback on user actions (e.g., successful submission, data loading) and smooth transitions between screens, enhancing the sense of a responsive and well-engineered 'operating system'.
